Salt Lake City Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Davis County Chamber of Commerce. This name represents the area the chamber operates and the community in which the chamber represents.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to give the Davis County community access to events, information and business connected to this specific chamber of commerce. It will allow those who seek to take part in the organization access to the membership application, explain benefits of participation, and encourage those who want to promote community development and economic growth to apply.

Scenarios

  • What business are currently members of the Davis County Chamber of Commerce?
  • Will the chamber be hosting any events this month within my community?
  • What are the goals of the Davis County Chamber of Commerce?
  • What are the benefits included with membership in the Davis County Chamber of Commerce?
